# 6. Progression — Funding & Upgrades

## 6.1 Currencies

### Reputation ⭐
- **Earned from:** completed samples, published papers (delayed, planned), successful proposals
- **Determines:** yearly funding grant amount, available proposal quality, Source Upgrade eligibility
- **Lost from:** unfulfilled committed proposals (−8 per proposal with 0 samples)

### Funding 💰
- **Starting amount:** 400 per run
- **Year-end grant:** 400 + (reputation × 3)
- **Carryover cap:** max 200 can carry from year to year (use it or lose most of it)
- Spent in the upgrade shop between cycles. Cannot be earned during gameplay — only via grant.
