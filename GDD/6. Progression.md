# 6. Progression — Funding & Upgrades

## 6.1 Currencies

### Reputation ⭐
- **Earned from:** completed samples, published papers (delayed, planned), successful proposals
- **Determines:** yearly funding grant amount, available proposal quality, Source Upgrade eligibility
- **Lost from:** unfulfilled committed proposals (−8 per proposal with 0 samples)

### Funding 💰
- **Starting amount:** 200K per run
- **Year-end grant:** 200K + (reputation × 2K)
- **Carryover cap:** max 100K can carry from year to year (use it or lose most of it)
- Spent in the upgrade shop between cycles. Cannot be earned during gameplay — only via grant.

## 6.2 Upgrade Shop (Cycle End)

All upgrades are **persistent across cycles and years**. Purchased upgrades show ✓ OWNED and cannot be repurchased (except stackable ones).

Year 1 is a tutorial run — the shop is limited to accessible, easy-to-understand upgrades. More structural upgrades unlock from year 2 onwards.

### Unlock Schedule

| Upgrade | Available from | Effect | Cost | Max |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Faster Prep | Y1C1 | Prep processing time −5% | 💰 10K | 1× |
| Faster Measurement | Y1C1 | Measurement time −5% | 💰 50K | 1× |
| Hire Postdoc | Y1C1 | +1 postdoc NPC helper (Level 1 AI — see §6.4) | 💰 100K | 2× |
| Prep Room × 2 | Year 2 | Prep Table handles 2 samples simultaneously | 💰 150K | 1× |
| Ring Maintenance | Year 2 | Restores +5% ring stability per cycle (net +3%/cycle after −2 decay) | 💰 80K | 1× |

## 6.4 Postdoc Intelligence Levels

Postdocs use a **level-based AI system** that improves with upgrades. Each postdoc has three states: `idle`, `moving`, `working`.

### Level 1 — Follow & Takeover *(current implementation)*

| Behaviour | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Default** | Random wandering inside the prep ring |
| **Trigger** | Player enters a room AND starts an action (deposit sample, begin experiment setup, start measurement) |
| **Response** | Postdoc walks to the room at player speed |
| **Takeover** | Once arrived, postdoc "takes over" — the action continues even after the player leaves the room |
| **Scope** | Prep rooms, Experimental Hutch (experiment setup), Control Room (measurement) |
| **Limitation** | Cannot initiate actions — only takes over player-started ones |

Only one postdoc is assigned per room. Labels: `idle` → `▶ helping` → `⚙ working`.

### Level 2 — Autonomous *(implemented)*

| Behaviour | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Default** | Random wandering (same as Level 1) |
| **Trigger** | Any active prep slot or started measurement — **no player presence required** |
| **Response** | Postdoc walks to the station autonomously |
| **Takeover** | Keeps the action ticking (same as Level 1) |
| **Scope** | Prep rooms, Control Room. Experiment setup still requires player to initiate |
| **Unlocked via** | Between-cycle action: "🎓 Train Postdoc" (see §6.5) |

### Level 3+ — *(future design space)*

Potential: multi-step task chaining, sample pickup/delivery, cross-beamline coordination.

## 6.5 Between-Cycle Actions

At the end of each cycle, the player must choose **one free action** before continuing. The Continue button is locked until an action is selected.

| Action | Effect | Availability |
|---|---|---|
| 📄 Write Paper | +reputation (`10 + samples_done × 2`, capped at 30) | Always |
| 🎓 Train Postdoc | Upgrades postdoc AI level (1→2) | Requires hired postdoc, not already max level |

This is separate from the funding-based upgrade shop — no cost, purely a strategic choice.
