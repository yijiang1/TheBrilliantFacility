# 7. Facility Eras — Source Upgrades

The facility begins as a **1st-generation synchrotron** and advances through four historical eras of synchrotron science. Advancing to a new generation unlocks new techniques and application types, and resets ring stability. This is the **primary long-term goal** of each run.

> Source Upgrade mechanics (UI, cost, trigger) are planned but not yet implemented in the prototype. Generation progression is currently year-gated (see table below).

## Era Table

| Gen | Historical Period | Game Years | New Techniques Added | Ring Stability Reset |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1st Gen | 1960s – 1970s | 1 – 3 | Powder Diffraction, X-ray Fluorescence, Laue Diffraction, X-ray Topography | Starts at 98% |
| 2nd Gen | 1970s – 1980s | 4 – 6 | EXAFS / XANES, SAXS, Protein Crystallography | Resets to 90% |
| 3rd Gen | 1990s – 2010s | 7 – 10 | Micro-CT, Micro-XRF, XRD Analysis | Resets to 95% |
| 4th Gen | 2020s – present | 11+ | Ptychography, Serial Crystallography, Phase Contrast, Time-Resolved XAS, Coherent Imaging | Resets to 100% |

## Historical Context

| Gen | Source Technology | Key Characteristic |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | Bending magnet radiation from particle physics accelerators (parasitic use) | Low brilliance, limited beamtime access |
| 2nd | Dedicated bending magnet storage rings | Stable, purpose-built, broader user community |
| 3rd | Undulator and wiggler insertion devices | 10,000× brighter than 2nd gen; routine nanoscale science |
| 4th | Diffraction-limited storage rings (DLSR) | Near-perfect coherence; atomic-resolution imaging |

## Beamline Technique System

Each generation adds new techniques to the **available pool**. At the start of each year, **4 techniques are randomly picked** from all techniques unlocked so far and assigned to the 4 beamlines. This means:

- In Gen 1, the 4 beamlines always cover all 4 Gen 1 techniques.
- From Gen 2 onward, there are more techniques than beamlines — the player will not see every technique every year, adding strategic variety.
- Proposal techniques are always drawn from the currently assigned beamline set.

## Source Upgrade Effects (Planned)

Beyond resetting ring stability and unlocking new science, a Source Upgrade will:

- Reduce all experiment durations by **30%** (brighter beam = faster scans)
- Increase the rate of high-reputation proposal arrivals
- Visually transform the facility (new colour palette, equipment art, HUD skin)
- Change the audio layer (richer soundtrack, new ambient sounds)
- Feel like reaching Act 2 in Slay the Spire — a genuine celebration moment
