# 10. Open Design Questions

To be resolved during M2–M4 development.

- **Publication Delay Detail:** Should papers from earlier cycles publish as a "notification" during gameplay (interrupting the player) or silently at the Cycle End screen? The former is more impactful; the latter is less distracting.

- **Stress / Burnout Meter:** Should the player character have a personal stamina bar? Thematic but potentially punishing for new players.

- **Second Hutch Scope:** Should the second hutch be a separate room requiring a new movement route, or a parallel slot within the existing hutch space?

- **Narrative Layer:** Emails, voicemails, cutscenes between cycles? Or stay purely mechanical?

- **Outreach / Demo Mode:** A no-fail-state version for science communication events?

- **Mobile:** Phaser.js prototype runs in mobile browsers. Is mobile a target platform?

- **Real Facility Collaboration:** Would any synchrotron (APS, Diamond, ESRF, ALS, Sirius) want co-branding or technical consulting?
